# 🛒 Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

An end-to-end analytics project built using Python, SQL, and Power BI to uncover customer purchasing behavior, revenue patterns, and product performance insights from shopping transaction data.

---

# 📌 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to transform raw customer shopping data into actionable business intelligence that can support:

- Customer segmentation
- Revenue optimization
- Product performance tracking
- Subscription behavior analysis
- Marketing strategy decisions
- Inventory planning

---

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## Revenue Analysis
- Male customers contributed 67.8% of total revenue
- Female customers contributed 32.2% of total revenue

## Customer Segmentation
- Loyal customers represented 80% of the customer base
- Returning customers formed 18%
- New customers represented 2.1%

## Subscription Insights
- Subscribers and non-subscribers showed nearly identical average spending
- Subscription increased loyalty more than spending

## Product Insights
- Identified top-performing products in each category
- Determined highest-rated products for quality benchmarking

## Age Group Insights
- Young Adults generated the highest total spending
- Middle-aged customers followed closely behind
